## Project Overview

RFM-based customer segmentation dashboard enabling marketing teams to target the right customers with personalized campaigns.

### Methodology

Applied Recency, Frequency, and Monetary (RFM) analysis to segment 500K+ customers into actionable groups:

- **Champions**: High value, frequent, recent buyers
- **Loyal Customers**: Regular purchasers with consistent spending
- **At Risk**: Previously active customers showing decline
- **Hibernating**: Long-inactive customers requiring reactivation

### Features

- Interactive segment exploration
- Customer journey visualization
- Cohort analysis with retention curves
- Campaign performance tracking

### Results

- 35% improvement in email campaign ROI
- Identified 12,000 at-risk high-value customers for retention outreach
