## Project Overview

This comprehensive sales dashboard provides executive-level insights into regional performance metrics across all business units.

### Key Features

- **Real-time Data Refresh**: Connects to SQL Server with scheduled refresh every 4 hours
- **Drill-Through Analysis**: Click any region to explore detailed performance breakdown
- **Dynamic Filtering**: Filter by date range, product category, and sales representative
- **Mobile Optimized**: Responsive design for tablet and phone viewing

### Technical Implementation

The dashboard leverages advanced DAX calculations for:

- Year-over-year growth comparisons
- Rolling 12-month averages
- Quota achievement percentages
- Trend forecasting with confidence intervals

### Impact

- Reduced executive reporting time by 60%
- Enabled data-driven decision making for regional managers
- Identified $2.3M in untapped market opportunities
