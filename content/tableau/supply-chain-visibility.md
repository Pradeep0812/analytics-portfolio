## Project Overview

End-to-end supply chain visibility solution providing real-time insights into inventory, suppliers, and logistics.

### Key Capabilities

- **Inventory Monitoring**: Real-time stock levels across 50+ warehouses
- **Supplier Scorecard**: Performance tracking with SLA compliance metrics
- **Demand Forecasting**: ML-powered predictions with 95% accuracy
- **Alert System**: Automated notifications for stockout risks

### Technical Architecture

Built on modern data stack:
- Fivetran for data ingestion
- Snowflake for data warehouse
- dbt for transformations
- Tableau for visualization

### Business Impact

- Reduced stockouts by 70%
- Improved supplier on-time delivery by 15%
- Saved $1.2M annually in holding costs
